WHO ARE WE?

At Umeme Sense, we specialize in renewable energy data solutions that generate valuable Renewable Energy Credits (RECs). Our smart metering technology accurately tracks clean energy production, providing verifiable data that can be converted into tradable credits. By quantifying renewable energy generation, we help businesses monetize their sustainability efforts.

Our comprehensive platform offers detailed analytics, certification tracking, and marketplace integration for RECs. We enable organizations to not only reduce their carbon footprint but also create new revenue streams through credit trading. Umeme Sense bridges the gap between renewable energy production and financial incentives, making sustainability profitable.

Our Mission

To revolutionize renewable energy data production by providing comprehensive monitoring and verification systems that enable users to generate, track, and monetize Renewable Energy Credits (RECs) and certificates. We empower individuals and organizations to transform their clean energy production into valuable, tradeable assets through accurate data collection and transparent certification processes.

Our Vision

To create a global ecosystem where every kilowatt-hour of renewable energy is accurately measured, verified, and rewarded. We envision a future where renewable energy producers, from residential solar panels to large wind farms, can seamlessly earn and trade RECs, driving widespread adoption of clean energy through financial incentives and data-driven transparency.
